DISTILLERY

SPIRITS

At Junction 35, we offer a variety of spirits including bourbon, rye whiskey, moonshine, vodka, gin and rum. We have hand-crafted a selection of specialty cocktails using our bold spirits to deliver a unique experience with our spirits.


View our selection of spirits and their stories below.

Moonshine

Our very own “white dog whiskey” is the base of all of our spirits and is known for its Cinnamon and Vanilla notes. With a distiller who says anything under 70 proof is just a mixed drink we bring you our Original Moonshine at 100 proof and all flavors at 70 proof.

Don’t let the proof full ya, our moonshine is smooth as silk and made with natural flavors! Our full shine lineup includes: Peach, Apple Pie, Blackberry, Strawberry, Margarita and Sweet Tea.

Rum

Our Puerto Rican Style Rum is Full Flavored & Smells like citrus & Vanilla. It’s got a warm taste with cream sugar and citrus notes. Summertimes favorite cocktail addition!

Gin

Our gin is one-of-a-kind in the gin world with a taste described as Crisp and Airy with a hint of floral and orange peels, made with Juniper & 6 other botanicals that are locally sourced….its the most refreshing sip to hit The Smokies!

Vodka

J35 Vodka is a non GMO Corn/Wheat/Barley product. Coming off the still distilled 5 times and filtered 12, this non sugar spirit is one of our best sellers of our lineup!

For our flavored spirit lovers check out our Citrus Vodka (Combo of lemon, lime, and orange for a perfect citrus pairing) and Jalapeno Vodka (Spicy with authentic jalapeno flavor)

Straight Bourbon Whiskey

Our wheated whiskey is aged just over 2 years with a taste that’s described as a dark gold/copper with orange brass highlights. J35 Bourbon is slightly sweet with cereal and vanilla over some white pepper. A sweet softer bourbon on the palate with a kickass finish.

Straight Rye Whiskey

Our Rye Whiskey is aged 3 years and loaded with fruit and spice

Our Whiskey lineup is completed with our best selling Honey Whiskey, Cinnamon Whiskey, and newest addition Caramel Apple Whiskey
